KMIT Internship



The Kettle Moraine IT internship program is an oppertunity to work the technology help desk at Kettle Moraine High and be deployed to classrooms if nessery. You can work on making guides for equipment and computers in the need of software or hardware repair are shipped in from the districts elementry and middle schools. It is a great way to get expernece troubleshooting computer problems, filling out support tickits, replacing parts, and working with clients. I was in the program for four terms, one freshman, one junior, and two for my senior year.
